Welcome to Bionics HRMS:

Why choose bionics human capital?

Bionics human capital is a cutting-edge, cloud-based human resource management system (hrms) designed to streamline and optimize all aspects of hr management.From recruitment to payroll, our comprehensive solution covers is all, ensuring seamless integration and efficient management of your workforce.

KEY FEATURES

Recruitments

Simplify your hiring process with our robust recruitment module. Manage job postings, track applicants, and streamline the entire recruitment workflow

Employee Management

Maintain comprehensive employee records, manage roles and responsibilities, and ensure seamless communication within your organization

Attendance

Track employee attendance efficiently with our integrated attendance module. Support for biometric integration ensures accurate and reliable data.

Biometric Integration

Enhance security and accuracy with biometric integration for attendance and access control, ensuring precise tracking of employee hours.

Payroll

Automate payroll processing, manage salaries, deductions, and benefits with ease. Ensure timely and accurate payments to your employees

Training

Facilitate employee development with our training module. Schedule and track training sessions, ensuring your team stays up-to-date with necessary skills

Employee Self Service

Empower your employees with self-service capabilities. Allow them to apply for leave, view attendance data, check upcoming holidays, and more, directly from their mobile devices
